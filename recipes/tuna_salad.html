Tuna Salad

Description

I am obsessed with tuna salad. WOW!

Ingredients

  • 12 Oz Tuna, drained
  • 2 Tablespoons Minced Celery
  • 2 Tablespoons minced Red Onion, Soaked for 5 mins and drained
  • 1 Teaspoon Minced Flat Leaf Parsley
  • 1/3 Cup Mayonnaise
  • 1 Tablespoon Whole Grain Mustard
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • Freshly squeezed lemon juice

Steps

  1. In a small bowl break up the tuna with a fork
  2. Toss the tuna with the dry ingredients
  3. Add the wet ingredients and stir
  4. Season with pepper and lemon juice to taste
