Whole Wheat Bread

Description

Prepare this recipe 12 hours in advance of baking! Bread is tasty, yum!

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups of Whole Wheat Flour
  • 2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Active Dry Yeast
  • 2 Cups Water

Steps

  1. To begin, mix the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. add the water and stir until a smooth dough is formed.
  3. Let the dough rise overnight for 12-13 hours.
  4. Put your dutch oven+lid into you oven and preheat to 375
  5. Transfer the dough ball onto parchement paper and into the dutch oven
  6. Cook for 30 minutes with the lid then remove the lid and cook for an additional 15 minutes
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool on a rack
